Development Process

I approach every product with a strong focus on both functionality and user experience. From concept to deployment, I manage the full development lifecycle — including UI/UX design, Front-End and Back-End development, testing, and continuous iteration.

01.

UI/UX Design & Wireframing

Every project starts with research. I study user behavior and business needs to craft intuitive flows and experiences. Through wireframes and interactive prototypes, I turn complex ideas into clear, testable UI structures that align with your goals — validated by real user feedback and product testing.

02.

Front-End Development

I build fast, scalable, and responsive interfaces using React, Next.js, Vite, Svelte and Tailwind. I focus on performance, accessibility (a11y), and component-driven architecture — applying best practices like FSD, Atomic Design, and CI/CD. Whether it’s a crypto app or admin dashboard, my goal is always clear code and clean UX.

03.

Back-End Development

From building APIs to managing infrastructure, I handle all server-side logic using TypeScript, Node.js (Express) and databases like MongoDB, MySQL or Supabase. I follow SOLID principles, clean code patterns, and write with scalability in mind — ensuring data flows securely and efficiently across your entire system.

04.

Analytics & Ongoing Support

I implement custom analytics and A/B testing pipelines to give you actionable insights into user behavior and performance. After launch, I offer 7 days of free support — including bug fixing, minor updates, and performance monitoring — to ensure your product runs smoothly in the real world.
